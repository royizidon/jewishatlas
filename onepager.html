Jewish Atlas

Mapping Heritage. Preserving Culture. Connecting Jews Worldwide
Mission

Build the world's most comprehensive database of Jewish heritage sites, providing institutions, municipalities, and organizations with a reliable, continuously updated infrastructure for education, tourism, planning, and community engagement.

Knowledge is Power. By documenting and mapping Jewish communities and heritage sites, we ensure their preservation and accessibility for generations to come.

License & Sponsorship

Licensing: Subscription-based access for organizations, municipalities, museums, and institutions. Licenses include live data access, embedding rights, customization options, and continuous updates.

Sponsorship: Companies and mission-aligned partners may support the documentation and preservation of Jewish heritage worldwide. Sponsorship enables open public access while supporting research, data verification, and long-term sustainability.

  • Recognition on the Jewish Atlas website and relevant project pages
  • Association with cultural preservation and education
  • Visibility to global Jewish communities and institutions
  • Optional acknowledgement by region, theme, or initiative
