Mission

Jewish Atlas builds enduring digital infrastructure for Jewish heritage worldwide — documenting sites at risk and enabling reliable, cross-border access to Jewish sites, communities, and institutions.

Jewish Atlas exists to ensure that Jewish sites, memory, and presence remain visible, discoverable, and connected to communities — through rigorous documentation and long-term stewardship.
