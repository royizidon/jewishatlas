About The Jewish Atlas

Our Mission

The Jewish Atlas aims to collect spatial data on Jewish sites from antiquity to today—synagogues, yeshivas, events, homes, and more.

Help us build this living archive by uploading new locations and using this platform to explore Jewish heritage globally.

Why This Matters

  • • Preserve Jewish culture
  • • Support education & research
  • • Help travelers explore Jewish life
